ABOUT THE ARTISTS

Michael Salvatori

Is an American composer known for his collaboration with colleague Martin O'Donnell on video game developer Bungie's series.

Together with O'Donnell, he composed the soundtrack to the 2014 video game, Destiny for which he won the 2014 Original Dramatic Score.

Martin O'Donnell

Is an American composer known for his collaboration with colleague Michael Salvatori on video game developer Bungie's series.

Together with O'Donnell, he composed the soundtrack to the 2014 video game, Destiny for which he won the 2014 Original Dramatic Score.

C. Paul Johnson

C. Paul Johnson, also known as C Paul, is a sound designer and musician at Bungie Studios. He was made a full-time Bungie staff member in September 2004.

Paul McCartney

Is an English musician, singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and composer. He is best known as a member of the famous rock band The Beatles.

In July 2012, McCartney and Bungie confirmed that McCartney, along with Martin O'Donnell and Michael Salvatori, is working on the soundtrack and score for Destiny.

Skye Lewin

Is a musician, composer, songwriter, record producer, and music editor.

currently serves as Music Director and Composer at Bungie and is a co-founder of the record label and music production company Sound Chemistry.
